Coin Tokenomics System

Powering Your Journey

The game's token plays a pivotal role in upgrading vessels, enhancing territorial holdings, procuring advanced armory, compensating your crew, and more.

Our innovative approach prioritizes a governance token over immediate NFT integration, ensuring sustained intrinsic value within our game's ecosystem.

Earning Tokens
  • Defeat other players in battle to earn tokens
  • Conquer enemy lands to steal their tokens
  • Upgrade your land to include a farm for additional token farming
Token Burning
  • A percentage of your tokens will be burned if defeated by other players, creating scarcity and value retention
  • Tokens used for ship upgrades, land upgrades, crew upgrades, and more are permanently retired from circulation.

FAQS

  • What sets Unknown Voyage apart from other mobile games?

    Unknown Voyage stands out due to its seamless integration of cryptocurrency, offering both entertainment and financial opportunities. Its focus on medieval exploration, conquests, and engaging PvP dynamics creates a unique and immersive gaming experience.

  • How does the game's cryptocurrency integration work?

    Instead of immediately integrating NFTs, Unknown Voyage prioritizes a governance token. This token, deeply embedded in the game's ecosystem, holds consistent intrinsic value, ensuring a symbiotic relationship between in-game utility and external value.

  • What benefits does owning land in Unknown Voyage offer?

    Land ownership in the game yields passive token farming, rewarding players daily for territorial ownership. It's a pivotal aspect contributing to the overall strategy and prosperity within the game.

  • How does PvP (Player-versus-Player) interaction affect gameplay?

    PvP encounters are strategic and impactful. Losing in ship-to-ship battles results in a transfer of 90% of the held tokens to the victor, while the remaining 10% are permanently removed from circulation, intensifying the value dynamics within the game.

  • What is the primary goal of Unknown Voyage?

    The primary objective is to uncover treasures through battles, enabling ship upgrades, and expanding territories. Players engage in strategic battles, aim for conquests, and fortify their holdings.

  • How often will the game receive updates after launch?

    Post-launch, Unknown Voyage is committed to regular monthly updates. These updates are intended to expand gameplay, introduce new features, and ensure ongoing balance adjustments for an enhanced gaming experience.

  • How do tokens function within the game?

    Tokens serve multiple purposes, including upgrading vessels, enhancing territorial holdings, and procuring advanced armory. Tokens used for upgrades are permanently removed from circulation through a burn mechanism.

  • Is there a specific strategy for success in Unknown Voyage?

    Success in the game involves a combination of strategic PvP engagements, land acquisition, and smart token management. Balancing territorial conquests, battles, and token utilization plays a crucial role in advancing within the game.

  • When can we expect the full launch of Unknown Voyage?

    We are diligently working towards a full launch in the near future. As part of our progress, we have successfully completed internal beta testing, ensuring that the game meets our quality standards before its official release. Stay tuned for updates on our official launch date!
